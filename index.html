Design till death. Become the ultimate tool to execute what you feel compelled to make. Never stop making. The clock will stop but you don’t have to. Art is not limited by hours worked. Art is experience. You are a collage—a collection of everything you’ve learned since your eyes opened—don’t waste it. Let go of what the thing should be, or who you think you are, and let them become what they become. If it’s not working, perhaps it isn’t meant to.
